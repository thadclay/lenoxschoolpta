October Membership Drive

If you join the Lenox PTA in the month of October, in addition to all of the other
benefits:

  • The class with 100% participation (including the teacher) or the class with the largest amount of joiners will receive a pizza party
  • You and your child(ren) will be recognized during morning announcements on
    Fridays
  • You will have a chance to be selected (through a raffle) for RESERVED PRIME SEATING at the Lenox Winter Concert

Membership dues for the year are $15.00 for the 1st family member and $5.00 for each additional member residing in the same household.

Register online and pay online with a credit card at https://lenoxpta.memberhub.store.
