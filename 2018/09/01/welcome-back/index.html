Welcome Back To School

On behalf of the PTA, it is our pleasure to welcome you back to school. If you are new parents, we welcome you and look forward to the opportunity to work with you. Over the summer, the PTA and the Executive Board had been working diligently to plan a variety of programs and activities that support our children’s education and enable them to be successful in school and in life.

The PTA goals for the 2018-2019 school year are:

  • To provide our children with the knowledge, skills and character necessary to become productive members of society through educational enrichment programs.
  • To strengthen the cooperation between parents, teachers, administrators and the community in the academic, physical, and social education of our children.

We invite you to join us in achieving these goals. The PTA offers many ways in which you can become involved.

Complete your membership forms and sign up for the Three for Me Promise. Feel free to contact any of the PTA officers with any questions and/or suggestions. We welcome everyone’s input and participation.

We look forward to working with you. With your help and involvement, we can enhance the educational experience at Lenox School and thereby make a difference in the lives of our children.
