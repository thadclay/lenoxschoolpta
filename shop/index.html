AmazonSmile

Who doesn’t like to shop Amazon?

Shop smile.amazon.com and AmazonSmile will make a donation to the Lenox PTA. Fundraising has never been simpler.

You can now shop on your favorite online shopping website and support your Lenox School PTA. A portion of eligible purchases will fund our student programs and events. It is with those same programs and events that earned us the National PTA “2018-2020 National School of Excellence” Award.

What is AmazonSmile?

AmazonSmile is a website operated by Amazon that lets customers enjoy the same wide selection of products, low prices, and convenient shopping features as on Amazon.com. The difference is that when customers shop on AmazonSmile, smile.amazon.com, the AmazonSmile Foundation will donate 0.5% of the price of eligible purchases to the charitable organizations selected by customers.

How does AmazonSmile work?

When first visiting smile.amazon.com, customers are prompted to log in. If it is not already selected, you will need to select a charitable organization in order to browse or shop at AmazonSmile. Please search and select Lenox PTA in Baldwin, NY. That’s it. With all your eligible purchases, the AmazonSmile Foundation will donate 0.5% of the purchase price to our Lenox PTA.

Parents, Teachers, Administrators, friends and families can shop and support Lenox. It’s simple.
